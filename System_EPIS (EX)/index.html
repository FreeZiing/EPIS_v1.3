F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is the purpose of E.P.I.S.?

    E.P.I.S. or Earthquake Preparedness Interactive System is to promote earthquake awareness through simple animation story.

  • What device can I access the system?

    E.P.I.S. can be accessed through smartphones, laptop and tablet

  • Is Duck, Cover and Hold useful?

    Yes, it protects you from falling object in debris during an earthquake

  • Why eathquake as the chosen disaster?

    The Philippines is vulnerable to earthquakes and volcanic eruptions produced by tectonic plate movement due to its placement on the Pacific Ring of Fire.

  • What software did you use for animation?

    The team used Macromedia Flash 8 for animation

  • Is this out for public?

    Currently Yes, as of this moment it is currently deployed through online
